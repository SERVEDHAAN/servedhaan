About Us

We serve the needy with your donations. Donating through us makes your job simple and easy. We make sure that your donations reach the needy. The entire donation process is transparent to the donar. Here you can donate whatever you want


Service

Upload the pics of the donating objects on our website. Add your address. Our service boys will collect your donations at your door step. If the quality of the objects is good then it will be directly donated to the needy. On the other hand, if the quality is poor then it will be sent to recycling and the recycled product will be uploaded in the sales section on our website. The recycled products on our website are at feasible / reasonable price.
